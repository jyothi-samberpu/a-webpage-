Farming is the process of cultivating land and raising livestock to produce food and raw materials.

It's a major part of agriculture, which also includes plant science. Here are some things to know about farming: History People have been practicing agriculture for thousands of years. Agriculture was a key factor in the rise of human civilization, as farming created food surpluses that allowed people to live in cities. Farmers Farmers can raise field crops, orchards, vineyards, poultry, or other livestock. They can own the land or work as laborers on land owned by others. Global action plans The UN Decade of Family Farmingprovides guidance for supporting family farmers. The plan recommends a series of actions that can be taken at the local and global level.
